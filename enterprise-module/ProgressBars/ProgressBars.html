Progress bar

Basic CSS for loading, redirecting, or action status.

  • Basic
  • Striped
  • Animated

Basic

Following the eXo Platform style, the blue color of the progress bar is darker than Bootstrap. It is known as electric blue.

<div class="progress">
    <div class="bar" style="width: 60%;"></div>
</div>

Striped

<div class="progress progress-striped">
    <div class="bar" style="width: 20%;"></div>
</div>

Animated

Add .active to .progress-striped to animate the stripes from left to right. It is not available in Internet Explorer.

<div class="progress progress-striped active">
    <div class="bar" style="width: 45%"></div>
</div>
