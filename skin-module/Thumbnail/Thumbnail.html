Thumbnail

How to display a thumbnail for an image attachment or a file in a folder?

  • Thumbnail for image attachments
  • Thumbnail in folders

Thumbnail for image attachments

A thumbnail with rounded borders can be used for image attachments in eXo Platform, such as topics, questions, and more. The thumbnail height is fixed at 80px, and its width is auto-resized to the original size of image.

When you hover over a thumbnail, two options, including View and Download, will be displayed for you to select.

Thumbnail in folders

The thumbnail is used for all content types in a folder and will be displayed in the same container. For image type, the thumbnail will be resized until its width or height obtains the maximum size.
